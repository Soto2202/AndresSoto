About Me

I’m Andres Soto, a professional orchestrator, arranger, and conductor with over 20 years of experience in symphonic music production. Based in Spain, I specialize in transforming musical ideas into fully orchestrated scores, working with tools like Finale, Sibelius, Dorico, and MuseScore. My mission is to provide musicians, composers, and creatives with accurate, expressive, and performance-ready orchestral materials.

Services

  • Orchestral Transcription: From audio or MIDI to full score and parts, with industry-standard notation software. Files delivered in PDF, MusicXML, and editable formats.
  • Orchestral Mockups: High-quality digital orchestrations using virtual instruments for realistic previews or demos.
  • Orchestration & Arranging: Custom orchestrations and arrangements for film, video games, stage works, and concert music.
