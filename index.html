Andrés Soto – Professional Orchestrator & Transcriber

Delivering symphonic excellence through orchestration, arrangement, and transcription.

About Me

I’m Andrés Soto, a professional orchestrator, arranger, and conductor with over 20 years of experience in symphonic music production. Based in Spain, I specialize in transforming musical ideas into fully orchestrated scores using Finale, Sibelius, Dorico, and MuseScore. My mission is to provide musicians and composers with accurate, expressive, and performance-ready orchestral materials.

Orchestration & Transcription Services

  • Orchestral Transcription: From audio or MIDI to full score and parts, using professional notation software. Deliverables include PDF, MusicXML, and source files.
  • Orchestral Mockups: Realistic digital orchestral previews using high-quality virtual instruments.
  • Custom Orchestration & Arranging: Tailored arrangements for film, video games, musicals, and concert pieces.
